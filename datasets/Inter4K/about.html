What is Inter4K?

Inter4K in a benchmark dataset for video interpolation and super-resolution. It scales over multiple video resolutions and frame rates with the goal of creating challenging benchmarks. Inter4K provides 1K video with a 80-10-10 train-validation-test split. All videos have durations of 5 seconds for standardized benchmarking.

What benchmarks can be measured by Inter4K?

  • Video frame interpolation
  • Video restoration
  • Video enhancement
  • Video super-resolution
  • Video denoising
